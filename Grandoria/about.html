Welcome to GAURIKEERTHANA RESIDENCY

At GAURIKEERTHANA RESIDENCY, we believe a stay in Guruvayoor should feel warm, simple, and homely.

Located just moments away from the sacred Guruvayoor Temple, we offer comfortable rooms with essential amenities. Whether you’re here for darshan, traveling with family, or visiting, our rooms are designed for convenience and peace of mind.

What makes us special is the care and attention – clean rooms, a friendly smile, and the comfort of feeling at home.
